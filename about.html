Charmaine Noten (º1989, Amsterdam, Netherlands) creates installations, performances and films, staging interventions in the public realm and in museum/theatre spaces.

She has a theatre background and trained as a theatrical performer at the youth trajectory at Likeminds an Amsterdam based theatre producer. She later went on to study Audiovisual Arts at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Ghent, Belgium and graduated in 2017.

Charmaine has worked for cultural institutions like De Balie, Frascati Productions en Tropenmuseum. Her work always tries to transcend the mundane. While tackling serious issues like social justice, religion and death, her work is saturated with optimism, naïvity and bad jokes.

Charmaine Noten currently lives and works in Amsterdam.
